Ancient India Timeline

  • 2500 BCE

    Indus Valley Civilization flourishes in the Indian subcontinent.

  • 1500 BCE

    Vedic period begins with the arrival of Indo-Aryan tribes.

  • 321 BCE

    Alexander the Great invades India.

  • 320 BCE

    Chandragupta Maurya establishes the Maurya Empire.

  • 185 BCE

    Pushyamitra Shunga overthrows the Maurya Empire and founds the Shunga dynasty.

  • 75 BCE

    Satavahana dynasty rises to power in Deccan India.

  • 320 CE

    Gupta Empire reaches its peak under Chandragupta II Vikramaditya.

  • 606 CE

    Harshavardhana establishes the Harsha Empire in northern India.

  • 1206 CE

    Delhi Sultanate is established by Qutb-ud-din Aibak.

  • 1526 CE

    Babur founds the Mughal Empire in India.
